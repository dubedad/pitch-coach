# Show the least-gameable proof you have

At pre-seed you rarely have revenue, so founders reach for vanity metrics (signups,
waitlist, LinkedIn views). Investors discount anything easy to inflate. Lead with the
*hardest* signal your stage can honestly offer.

Rough order, weakest → strongest:
- Signups / waitlist (easy to game) → survey intent → unpaid pilots → repeated usage →
  a letter of intent → someone paying money.

One paying customer or a signed LOI beats 10,000 waitlist emails. If you only have soft
signals, say so honestly and show the one hard thing you're chasing next.

Coaching question: *"Which of your proof points would survive a skeptic asking 'so what?'
— and which is just easy to inflate?"*
